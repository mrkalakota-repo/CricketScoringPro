Features

Everything your team needs

From the first ball to the final over — Inningsly handles scoring, stats, and communication.

🎯

Ball-by-Ball Scoring

Tap to score every delivery — runs, extras, wickets, and more. Unlimited undo keeps you accurate even mid-over.

👥

Team & Player Management

Build your squad with player profiles including batting style, bowling style, photos, and jersey numbers.

💬

Real-Time Team Chat

Message your team before and after matches. All players on a Pro team get access — no extra logins needed.

🏆

Leagues & Tournaments

Create round-robin leagues or knockout brackets. Track NRR, standings tables, and fixture schedules.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Your teams, matches, and stats are backed up to the cloud. Switch devices without losing a single ball.

🔗

Delegate Access

Share a 6-character code with a scorer. They get temporary editor access to your team — time-limited, single-use.

📊

Live Scorecard & Stats

Auto-generated scorecards with batting and bowling tables. Export and share after the match with one tap.

📍

Nearby Live Scores

See other teams scoring live near you. Follow the action in real time from the sideline — no account needed.

⚙️

Custom Match Formats

T20, ODI, Test, or fully custom — set your own overs, bowling limits, and powerplay rules for any format.

How it works

Up and scoring in minutes

No setup headaches. Create your account and start your first match right away.

1

Create Your Account

Register with your phone number. Verify with a one-time code — no email or password required.

2

Build Your Team

Add players with their details. Invite a scorer via delegate code or let any player join with yours.

3

Start the Match

Choose format, complete the toss, set your Playing XI, and tap to start scoring ball by ball.

4

Share the Scorecard

When the final wicket falls, share the full scorecard with your team and friends instantly.
